Project Description

The Hidden Fork will be a mobile app designed to help users discover and support local and small businesses. It will prioritize "hole-in-the-wall" restaurants over large chains, providing users with detailed information about local eateries, including menus, prices, social media links, and verified reviews from real customers. The app will feature a unique review system where customers can leave authenticated reviews through a one-time-use QR code. Some other features will be analytics to track customer visits and feedback. By focusing on local businesses, this app aims to create a platform where small businesses can thrive without being overshadowed by big corporations.
